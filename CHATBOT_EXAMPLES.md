## 🚀 How to Use These Examples

1. **Open** `lib/chatbot-config.ts`
2. **Copy** the Q&A pairs you want to use
3. **Add** them to the `INSTANT_QNA` array
4. **Customize** keywords and answers to match your actual content
5. **Test** locally with `npm run dev`

---

## 💡 Tips for Creating Your Own Q&A

- **Keywords:** Use 4-6 keywords per question (case-insensitive)
- **Answers:** Keep responses concise (1-2 sentences)
- **Variety:** Include keywords for synonyms ("email" vs "contact")
- **Real Problems:** Add Q&A for questions you actually get asked
- **Update Regularly:** Add new Q&A as your site evolves

---

## 📊 Recommended Minimum Set

At minimum, include these essential Q&A pairs:
- ✅ What is this website about?
- ✅ How can I contact you?
- ✅ Is the content free?
- ✅ How can I contribute?
- ✅ How do I navigate the site?
